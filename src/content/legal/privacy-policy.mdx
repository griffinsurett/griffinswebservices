## {++sectionNumber}. Introduction

Welcome to {siteData.title}. We respect your privacy and are committed to 
protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we 
collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

## {++sectionNumber}. Information We Collect

When you interact with {siteData.title}, we may collect:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, and phone number when you submit forms or contact us.
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited (via cookies when you consent).
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with our site, pages viewed, time spent, and referral sources (via cookies when you consent).

## {++sectionNumber}. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain our services
- To respond to your inquiries and requests
- To improve our website and user experience
- To analyze site performance and usage (with consent)
- To send marketing communications (with consent)

## {++sectionNumber}. Third-Party Services

With your consent, we may share information with:

- **Google Translate:** For website translation (functional cookies)
- **Google Analytics:** For site analytics (performance cookies, if enabled)
- **Advertising Networks:** Facebook, Google Ads (targeting cookies, if enabled)

We use **Vercel** for hosting. Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights are enabled on this site and automatically collect the following on every page load — without cookies and without your name or email address:

- Web performance metrics (page load time, Core Web Vitals)
- Browser type and device category
- Country-level approximate location (derived from IP; IP address is not stored)
- Referring URL and page path

This data is used solely to monitor and improve site performance. It cannot be used to identify you individually. There is no opt-out for Vercel Analytics as it operates at the infrastructure level with no personal data collected. For details, see [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).

### AI-Powered Features (OpenAI)

Our website offers a **Website Chat Assistant** powered by the **OpenAI API**:

- **What is sent to OpenAI:** When you interact with the chat assistant, the messages you type are sent to OpenAI's servers to generate responses. This data is processed by OpenAI subject to their [Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy) and [Terms of Use](https://openai.com/terms). We do not use your inputs to train AI models.
- **What we store:** Your conversation (the messages you send and the assistant's replies) is stored on our infrastructure (Supabase) under a random, per-visit session identifier so we can maintain conversation context and improve support. It is **not** linked to your name, email, or IP address unless you choose to type such details into the chat. Please avoid sharing sensitive personal information in the chat.

We use OpenAI's `gpt-4o-mini` model (and `text-embedding-3-small` for retrieving relevant site content) and apply rate limiting and input validation to minimize unnecessary data transmission. By using this feature, you acknowledge that your inputs are processed by OpenAI and stored as described above.

### Appointment Scheduling (Calendly)

We use **Calendly** to facilitate call bookings. When you click "Book a Free Call" or schedule an appointment, you are directed to Calendly's platform. Any information you provide during scheduling (name, email, availability) is collected and stored by Calendly subject to their [Privacy Policy](https://calendly.com/privacy) and [Terms of Use](https://calendly.com/terms). We receive your booking details via Calendly's notification system.
