## What Hosting Covers

### Stable hosting

We keep the site live on solid hosting so you are not stuck dealing with outages, random renewals, or server-level technical problems on your own.

### Updates and maintenance

Software, plugins, packages, and routine maintenance stay handled so the site does not quietly fall behind until something breaks.

### Backups and recovery

Backups are part of the setup, so if something goes wrong there is a clean path to restore the site instead of scrambling after the fact.

### Support from the same team

You are not bouncing between a host, a developer, and a generic support queue. The same team handles the technical side and can actually fix what changed.

## Who This Fits Best

This is a strong fit for businesses that do not want to manage the website themselves, do not want to chase multiple vendors, and want one team to keep the site online and current after launch.

## Why Businesses Keep This With Us

Good hosting is not just server space. It is the ongoing layer that keeps the site available, current, and easier to trust over time.
